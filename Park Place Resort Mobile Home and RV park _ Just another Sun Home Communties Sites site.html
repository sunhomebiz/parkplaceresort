Welcome to Park Place Resort Mobile Home & RV Park, Meza AZ

Thank you so much for considering Park Place Resort a 55+ community in the Valley of the Sun for your winter home. We are located in Mesa Arizona just minutes from great shopping and dining. Walking through the park you will see the Superstition Mountains just a short drive away.

We cater to 55 plus adults by offering weekly, monthly, seasonal and year-round rates. Most of our seniors who visit East Mesa are here for at least a few months. Our resort feels like an extended family community, for active seniors, with such planned activities as socials, classes, excursions, shuffleboard, mini golf and tours. Many renters decide to buy one of our mobile homes/park models after developing new wonderful friendships.

We encourage RVer’s  to make their reservations at least a couple of months in advance. In fact, we are sometimes booked up a year in advance in winter.

The friendliest park in the valley!

Our winter visitors are some of the friendliest folks around, in fact Park Place Resort is rated as the friendliest MH Park in the valley over and over again.

The best place for your best days

It’s time to relax and enjoy life!  Enjoy all the excitement of Mesa’s attractions from the peaceful community at Park Place Resort to the hiking trails of the Superstition mountains.
